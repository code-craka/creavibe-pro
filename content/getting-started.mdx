# Getting Started with CreaVibe

Welcome to CreaVibe! This guide will help you get started with our AI-powered content creation platform.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [CreaVibe](https://CreaVibe) and click on the "Get Started" button
2. Fill in your details in the registration form
3. Verify your email address
4. Set up your profile

## Setting Up Your First Project

To create your first project:

1. Navigate to the Dashboard
2. Click on "New Project"
3. Choose a project type (Blog, Image, or Video)
4. Give your project a name
5. Start creating!

## Collaborating with Your Team

Invite team members to collaborate on your projects:

1. Go to Project Settings
2. Click on "Team Members"
3. Enter email addresses of team members
4. Assign appropriate roles (Editor, Viewer, Admin)

## Getting Help

If you need assistance, you can:

- Check our [Documentation](/docs)
- Contact support at support@CreaVibe
- Join our [Community Hub](/community)

We're excited to see what you'll create with CreaVibe!
